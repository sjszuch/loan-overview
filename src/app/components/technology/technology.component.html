SharePoint

When working on any project, it's critical to keep documents, presentations, and other forms of documentation organized. For this project, SharePoint was the clear choice for this. Wireframes, designs, and other references are all stored within the project SharePoint for easy access. Because there are always new hires and contractees being added to the project, it also makes for an easy overview of the project's past and current resources.

Microsoft Teams

Microsoft Teams is First National Bank's go-to platform for most communication purposes, especially within the development branch where frequent meetings, messages, and check-ins are a necessity. Because the team working on the project was spread across the world, it was important to choose a platform that provided stable service and allowed for as many features as possible to promote a productive team enviornment. The daily meetings are all hosted via Microsoft Teams, and the program's chat and calendar features make for a great all-in-one platform.
